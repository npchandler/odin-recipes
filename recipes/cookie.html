Cookies

cookies are pretty tasty ngl

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Microwave the butter for about 40 seconds.
  3. Beat the butter with the sugars until creamy.
  4. Add the vanilla and the egg and beat on low speed.
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Mix well, then add chocolate chips.
  7. Bake until the cookies look puffy and dry and just barely golden.
